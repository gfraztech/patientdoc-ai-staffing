Our Story of Clinical Excellence in Maryland

Rooted in the heart of Maryland, PatientDoc AI has been the bridge between world-class healthcare talent and the facilities that serve our community. We don't just fill shifts; we empower care.

Who We Are

Founded by healthcare veterans who recognized the critical need for reliable staffing in Maryland's diverse medical landscape, PatientDoc AI began as a small family-oriented project. Over two decades, we have evolved into a premier staffing partner for major hospital systems and private clinics alike.

Our journey is defined by a deep understanding of the local healthcare ecosystem, from the bustling trauma centers of Baltimore to the specialized rehabilitation facilities across the Chesapeake region.
